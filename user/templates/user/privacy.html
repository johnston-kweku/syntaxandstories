Community Guidelines

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Our Mission

Syntax and Stories is a community built on creativity, respect, and meaningful storytelling. These guidelines help ensure our platform remains a safe, welcoming, and inspiring space for writers and readers from all backgrounds.

Be Respectful

Treat all community members with kindness and respect, even when you disagree.

  • No harassment, bullying, or personal attacks
  • No hate speech, discrimination, or bigotry
  • Respect diverse perspectives and experiences
  • Provide constructive criticism, not destructive comments
  • Respect others' privacy and personal boundaries

Create Original Content

We celebrate original creativity and authentic voices.

  • Post only content you have created or have permission to share
  • Give proper credit when inspired by or referencing others' work
  • No plagiarism or copyright infringement
  • Be authentic and true to your voice

Keep Content Appropriate

While we support creative expression, certain content is not permitted:

  • No illegal content: Content that violates laws or promotes illegal activity
  • No explicit sexual content: Pornographic or sexually explicit material
  • No graphic violence: Excessively violent or gory content
  • No dangerous content: Instructions for harmful or dangerous activities
  • No misinformation: Deliberately false or misleading information
  • No self-harm promotion: Content encouraging self-harm or suicide

Note: Mature themes can be explored thoughtfully in storytelling with appropriate content warnings.

No Spam or Self-Promotion

  • Don't post repetitive or irrelevant content
  • No excessive self-promotion or advertising
  • No manipulating engagement (e.g., "like for like" schemes)
  • No posting the same content repeatedly
  • Keep comments relevant to the post

Protect Privacy

  • Don't share others' personal information without consent
  • Respect the privacy of minors
  • Don't impersonate others
  • Be mindful when sharing personal stories about others

Engage Constructively

Build a positive community through meaningful interactions:

  • Provide thoughtful feedback that helps others grow
  • Celebrate others' successes and milestones
  • Ask questions and engage in genuine discussions
  • Support new writers and welcome newcomers
  • Report content that violates guidelines

Content Warnings

When your content contains sensitive themes (violence, death, mental health issues, etc.), please use content warnings or tags to help readers make informed choices about what they read.

Reporting Violations

If you see content or behavior that violates these guidelines:

  • Use the report feature to flag the content
  • Provide specific details about the violation
  • Don't engage with or respond to harmful content
  • Our moderation team will review all reports

Consequences

Violations of these guidelines may result in:

  • Content removal
  • Warning messages
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent account termination
  • Reporting to law enforcement (for illegal activity)

We apply these consequences fairly and proportionally, taking into account the severity and frequency of violations.

Appeals

If you believe content was removed or your account was suspended in error, you may appeal the decision by contacting appeals@syntaxandstories.com . Include details about the specific content or action you're appealing.

Be Part of Something Special

Together, we're building a community where creativity thrives, diverse voices are heard, and meaningful connections are made through the power of storytelling. Thank you for being part of Syntax and Stories and helping make it a positive space for everyone.

Questions?

If you have questions about these Community Guidelines, please contact us at community@syntaxandstories.com
